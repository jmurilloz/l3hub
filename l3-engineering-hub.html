Engineering L3
Support & Operations

A dedicated engineering team bridging customer-facing support and the development organization — owning incident response, release communications, escalated issues, and operational metrics.

01 Mission

L3 coordinates incident response, owns incident and release communications, manages escalated issues, and governs the operational metrics that keep engineering, product, and the business aligned on what's happening — and what healthy looks like.

02 Where L3 Sits

L3 is a peer team to Development, CloudOps, QA Engineering, and PMO — inside the same engineering organization, not a tier below it.
